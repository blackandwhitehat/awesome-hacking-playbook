## Standards (for reference)

- [NMEA 0183](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NMEA_0183) - National Marine Electronics Association serial protocol
- [NMEA 2000](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NMEA_2000) - CAN bus-based marine instrument standard (based on SAE J1939)
- [IEC 61162](https://www.iec.ch/homepage) - International standard for maritime navigation and communication
- [ISO 11783 (ISOBUS)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_11783) - Tractors and machinery CAN bus (same foundation as NMEA 2000)
- [SAE J1939](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SAE_J1939) - Vehicle bus standard that NMEA 2000 is based on
