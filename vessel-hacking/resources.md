## Standards (for reference)

- **NMEA 0183**  -  National Marine Electronics Association serial protocol
- **NMEA 2000**  -  CAN bus-based marine instrument standard (based on SAE J1939)
- **IEC 61162**  -  International standard for maritime navigation and communication
- **ISO 11783**  -  Tractors and machinery CAN bus (same foundation as NMEA 2000)
